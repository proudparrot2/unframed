Unframed BETA

Unframed is the all-in-one solution to stop your site from being embedded, and stop people stealing your content.


Features

Here some features of Unframed:

• Unintrusive

Doesn't interfere with any current scripts that you have on your site, or your site content.

• Simple to setup

Unframed only takes one line of HTML to setup - you don't need to configure any files to get up and running.

• Support for same-domain embeds

You can still embed other pages from your site without getting blocked.

• Support for alternate links

If you have a situation where you distribute multiple links of your website, Unframed will have no problem handling them.

Installation

Install Unframed by inserting this script tag at the very bottom of your page:


<script src="https://unframed.netlify.app/latest.js"></script>

Why choose Unframed?

Unframed is trusted by some of the largest game sites avaiable. Compared to our competitors, such as Unbed, we don't require any external configuration to get up and running. Also compared to Unbed, any alternate links that you have setup will also work under Unframed.
