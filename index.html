Unframed

Unframed is the all-in-one solution to stop your site from being embedded, and stop people stealing your content.


Features

• Unintrusive

Doesn't interfere with any current scripts that you have on your site, or your site content.

• Simple to setup

Unframed only takes one line of HTML to setup - you don't need to configure any files to get up and running.

• Supports same-domain embeds

You can still embed other pages from your site without getting blocked.

• Use multiple links and about:blank

If you have a situation where you distribute multiple links of your website, Unframed will have no problem handling them - unlike Unbed, in which you have to manually insert each link. It also supports about:blank pages!


Installation

Install Unframed by inserting this script tag at the very bottom of your page:


<script src="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/proudparrot2/unframed/latest.js"></script>

Updating

Updating Unframed is extremely easy - you don't have to do anything! Compared to other competitors, like Unbed, updates are automatically applied when they are released. Support to revert to older versions will be added soon.
